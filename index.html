About Me

I am an undergraduate student studying computer science at McGill University and Montreal Institute of Learning Algorithms (MILA), supervised by Prof. Jackie Chi Kit Cheung.

My research interests span a wide range of areas within the field of artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on natural language processing. More specifically, I work on neural coreference resolution, which is a core task of natural language understanding and information retrieval. Recently, I am also interested in exploring and extending the capabilities of large language models. I publish my work on top-tier NLP conferences such as NAACL and COLING.
